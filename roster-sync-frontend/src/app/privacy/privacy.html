Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

What we collect

RosterSync collects your Google account name, email address, and OAuth tokens necessary to access your Google Calendar. We also store the roster URL you provide and a log of sync activity.

How we use it

Your data is used exclusively to synchronize your crew roster with your Google Calendar. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

Data storage

All data is stored in a secured database. OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only to write events to your calendar on your behalf.

Third parties

We use Google APIs to access your calendar. Your use of Google services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Data retention

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. All data is deleted immediately upon request.

Google API scopes

RosterSync requests access to your Google Calendar to create, update, and delete events on your behalf. No other Google data is accessed.
